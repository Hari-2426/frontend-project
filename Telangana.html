Telangana

Hyderabad

Hyderabad, the capital city of Telangana, is famous for Charminar, Golconda Fort, Ramoji Film City, and its delicious Hyderabadi Biryani.

  • ⏰ Best Time: October – February
  • 💰 Budget: ₹6,000 – ₹12,000 (3–4 days)
  • 👨‍💼 Guide: Available at tourist spots
  • 🌤️ Weather: Warm, 18°C – 32°C
  • 📍 Location: Telangana, India

Warangal

Warangal is famous for its historical Warangal Fort, Thousand Pillar Temple, and Ramappa Temple (UNESCO World Heritage Site).

  • ⏰ Best Time: October – March
  • 💰 Budget: ₹4,000 – ₹7,000 (2–3 days)
  • 👨‍💼 Guide: Not mandatory
  • 🌤️ Weather: Pleasant, 16°C – 30°C
  • 📍 Location: Telangana, India

Nizamabad

Nizamabad is known for its forts, ancient temples, and the Pocharam Wildlife Sanctuary.

  • ⏰ Best Time: November – February
  • 💰 Budget: ₹3,000 – ₹6,000 (2 days)
  • 👨‍💼 Guide: Not needed
  • 🌤️ Weather: Moderate, 18°C – 28°C
  • 📍 Location: Telangana, India

Khammam

Khammam is popular for Khammam Fort, Kinnerasani Dam, and the scenic Papi Hills on the Godavari River.

  • ⏰ Best Time: November – March
  • 💰 Budget: ₹3,500 – ₹6,500 (2 days)
  • 👨‍💼 Guide: Local guides available
  • 🌤️ Weather: Mild, 17°C – 29°C
  • 📍 Location: Telangana, India

Karimnagar

Karimnagar is known for its ancient temples like Vemulawada Raja Rajeshwara Swamy Temple and Lower Manair Dam.

  • ⏰ Best Time: October – February
  • 💰 Budget: ₹3,000 – ₹6,000 (2 days)
  • 👨‍💼 Guide: Not required
  • 🌤️ Weather: Pleasant, 15°C – 28°C
  • 📍 Location: Telangana, India
