Andhra Pradesh

Tirupathi

Tirupati is one of the most famous pilgrimage centers in India, home to the Sri Venkateswara Temple.

  • ⏰ Best Time: September – February
  • 💰 Budget: ₹5,000 – ₹8,000 (2 days)
  • 👨‍💼 Guide: Available near temple
  • 🌤️ Weather: Pleasant, 15°C – 25°C in winter
  • 📍 Location: Andhra Pradesh, India

Visakhapatnam

Vizag is a coastal city known for its beaches, Kailasagiri hills, Araku Valley nearby, and the famous Submarine Museum.

  • ⏰ Best Time: October – February
  • 💰 Budget: ₹5,000 – ₹9,000 (3 days)
  • 👨‍💼 Guide: City tour guides available
  • 🌤️ Weather: Tropical, 20°C – 32°C
  • 📍 Location: Andhra Pradesh, India

Srisailam

Srisailam is a famous pilgrimage destination, home to the Mallikarjuna Jyotirlinga temple and surrounded by Nallamala forests.

  • ⏰ Best Time: September – February
  • 💰 Budget: ₹4,000 – ₹7,000 (2 days)
  • 👨‍💼 Guide: Available near temple
  • 🌤️ Weather: Pleasant, 15°C – 28°C
  • 📍 Location: Kurnool district, Andhra Pradesh

Nellore

Nellore is famous for its historic temples, aquaculture, and delicious seafood cuisine. It is also home to the Penna River and the Sri Ranganathaswamy Temple.

  • ⏰ Best Time: October – March
  • 💰 Budget: ₹3,500 – ₹6,000 (2 days)
  • 👨‍💼 Guide: Not mandatory, locals are helpful
  • 🌤️ Weather: Warm & humid, 20°C – 32°C
  • 📍 Location: Andhra Pradesh, India

Vijayawada

Vijayawada, situated on the banks of the Krishna River, is famous for the Kanaka Durga Temple, Prakasam Barrage, and bustling city life. Amaravathi, the historical capital of Andhra Pradesh, is known for its ancient Buddhist Stupa and cultural heritage.

  • ⏰ Best Time: November – February
  • 💰 Budget: ₹5,000 – ₹9,000 (3 days)
  • 👨‍💼 Guide: Available near Kanaka Durga Temple
  • 🌤️ Weather: Moderate, 18°C – 32°C
  • 📍 Location: Krishna District, Andhra Pradesh
